Export to PNG & CSV

Share the plan outside Jira in two clicks. Export the timeline as a PNG image to drop into a status report or slide, or export the underlying tasks as a CSV — Key, Summary, Type, Status, Assignee, Start, End, Duration, Progress and Parent — for a spreadsheet or further analysis.

  • PNG captures the current chart view.
  • CSV exports the tasks currently shown (filters respected).
  • Both available from the toolbar “…” menu.
