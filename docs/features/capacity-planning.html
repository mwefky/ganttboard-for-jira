Capacity planning — see who’s over this week

Native Jira can’t tell you who’s overloaded. GanttBoard’s Capacity view sums each assignee’s workload for the week and flags exactly who is over capacity — so you can rebalance before the sprint slips, not after.

  • Per-assignee workload bars — one glance shows the whole team’s load for the week.
  • Over-capacity banner — names the people who are over and by how many days.
  • Click a person to drill in — see the exact tasks driving their load, and open any one in Jira.
  • No time-tracking add-on required — it works from the dates and estimates already on your issues.

It’s the lean version of an enterprise resource module — the 20% that answers “who’s overloaded this week?” without the price tag or the setup. Pair it with the Gantt timeline to move work off the overloaded people, and read the deeper guide on resource & capacity planning in Jira.
