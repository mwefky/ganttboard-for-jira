Auto-scheduling

Auto-schedule walks your dependency graph and shifts task dates so everything lines up — a successor never starts before its predecessor finishes (plus any lag). When something slips, one click cascades the impact through the rest of the plan instead of you dragging every downstream bar by hand.

  • Respects all four dependency types and lag.
  • Honours your working days and holidays.
  • Warns you about conflicts rather than silently breaking links.
