Inline editing (status, assignee, dates)

The spreadsheet table on the left of the split view is fully editable. Double-click a status cell to transition the issue through its real Jira workflow, a assignee cell to reassign, or a date cell to set start and due dates. Every edit writes back to Jira immediately — no separate sync step.

  • Status options come from your actual Jira workflow.
  • Assignee picker pulls the same users Jira does.
  • Filling in missing dates makes the bar appear on the timeline instantly.
