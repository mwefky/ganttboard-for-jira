Baselines: plan vs. actual

Save a baseline — a snapshot of your schedule at a point in time — then overlay it on the live chart to see how far the plan has drifted. It is the simplest way to answer “are we ahead or behind what we committed to?” without a spreadsheet.

  • Save unlimited named baselines.
  • Toggle the baseline overlay on or off.
  • Compare current dates against the saved plan visually.
