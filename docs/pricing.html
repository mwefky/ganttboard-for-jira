Pricing FAQ

Is it really free?

Yes. Through October 1, 2026, GanttBoard is 100% free for every team size — no credit card, no usage caps, no trial countdown. There is no asterisk.

What happens on October 1, 2026?

Paid tiers activate. Teams of 10 or fewer users stay on the free tier forever (matches the industry standard). Teams above 10 get a 30-day grace period to decide, then billing begins at $1.20/user/month for the 11–100 tier. All existing users are notified via the Atlassian Marketplace before any charge.

Do I get a refund if I don't like it?

The Atlassian Marketplace handles refunds under its standard policy: you can uninstall at any time and the prorated remainder of the current billing period is refunded automatically. There is no separate contract with us.

How is it different from BigGantt?

BigGantt is a Connect app built on external servers with a proprietary scheduling engine and a separate control panel. It charges $1.82/user/month at the 11–100 tier. GanttBoard is a native Forge app — your data stays inside Atlassian Cloud — and costs $1.20/user/month, roughly 34% less. Feature-wise you get critical path, baselines, 4 dependency types, and auto-scheduling in both, but GanttBoard adds facilitated standup mode, keyboard shortcuts, and the Runs on Atlassian badge.

Do you support Jira Data Center?

Not yet. GanttBoard is currently Jira Cloud only. A Data Center version is on the roadmap post-launch, once we have a meaningful Cloud install base. If Data Center is a hard requirement today, WBS Gantt or Structure.Gantt are established options.
